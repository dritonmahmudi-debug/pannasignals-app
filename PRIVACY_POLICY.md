# Privacy Policy for Panna Signals

**Last Updated**: December 15, 2025

## Introduction

Panna Signals ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- **Email address**: Used for authentication and account management
- **User ID**: Generated automatically for identification

### Usage Data
- **Trading signals viewed**: Which signals you view and interact with
- **Favorites**: Signals you mark as favorites
- **App analytics**: How you use the app (via Firebase Analytics)

### Device Information
- **Device type and OS version**: For compatibility and crash reporting
- **Push notification tokens**: To send you trading signal notifications
- **Crash logs**: To improve app stability (via Firebase Crashlytics)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain our service
- Send you trading signal notifications (with your consent)
- Improve app performance and fix bugs
- Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
- Process premium subscriptions (via Google Play Billing)

## Data Storage and Security

- **Firebase**: We use Google Firebase for authentication, analytics, and crash reporting
- **Encryption**: All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
- **Access Control**: Only authorized personnel can access user data
- **Data Retention**: We retain your data as long as your account is active

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Google Firebase**: Authentication, Analytics, Crashlytics, Cloud Messaging
- **Google Play Billing**: For premium subscription payments

These services have their own privacy policies:
- [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
- [Google Play Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Push Notifications

With your permission, we send push notifications for:
- New trading signals
- Important market updates
- Premium subscription confirmations

You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time.

## Premium Subscriptions

- Managed entirely through Google Play Billing
- We do not store your payment information
- Billing is handled by Google according to their [Terms of Service](https://play.google.com/intl/en_us/about/play-terms/)
